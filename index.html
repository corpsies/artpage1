WHO I AM

Corpsies was born at twelve years old and cultivated with crayons, oils, and acrylic paints over the years. From physical media, I moved on and taught myself how to use digital media – My iPad and Apple Pencil becoming my favored media type.

WHAT I DO

Mainly specializing in character creation, branding, and logos, my work has also appeared on a Rapper’s design line, whiskey bottles sold at local stores, and a mural in Oshkosh, WI.

Creating the identity of a character, company, or organization is both challenging and rewarding. A new creation can spring from an experiment in two-dimensional media and three-dimensional structure, or from a brainstorming session that produces only scribbles and doodles. Regardless of their intense pace or quieter moments, all of my creations come to life on paper with pencil, ink, and paint. I consider these works of art worthy of their individual stories and personalities; each with words to say and reason to live.

CURRENT PROGRESS

I've been a freelance artist for over five years, enjoying what I do every step of the way. An artist's dream – to have a job that's fun – is practically a reality. I enjoy the process of creating artwork – from rough sketches to final project – and love seeing my clients' reaction when they see their finished products. My work is all about making you, my client, smile. I love what I do, and there is nothing more satisfying than seeing one of my clients glow after receiving their new logo or merchandise. I have been told many times by my clients that they are "speechless" which, to me, is an incredible feeling. Please take a look around my portfolio and get in touch with me for any questions.
